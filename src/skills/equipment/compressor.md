# Air Compressor — Skill Card

## Key concepts
- **Specific Energy Consumption (SEC)** — kWh/m³ delivered air at standard pressure
- **Unloaded power** — modulating compressor uses 30–70% of full-load even when not producing air
- **Pressure drop** through filters, dryers, piping — every 1 bar drop ≈ 7% extra energy
- **Heat of compression** — recoverable: ~70–80% of input as heat for water/space heating
- Types: rotary screw (most common), reciprocating, centrifugal

## Common issues in Thai factories
- **Air leaks**: typical factory loses 20–40% of compressed air through leaks
  - Quick test: shut down production, measure compressor cycle frequency
- Pressure setpoint too high (drop 1 bar → save ~7%)
- Multiple compressors running unloaded
- Inlet air temp high (every 4°C cooler → ~1% more capacity)
