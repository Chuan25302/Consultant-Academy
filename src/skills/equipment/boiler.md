# Boiler — Skill Card

## Key concepts
- **Steam quality**: dryness fraction; wet steam carries water → lower enthalpy at point of use
- **Blowdown**: removes dissolved solids; too much = wasted energy, too little = scaling
- **Stack temp + O₂**: every 1% excess O₂ ≈ 1% efficiency loss; stack >250°C = recoverable heat
- **Condensate return**: every 1°C of returned condensate avoided = ~0.2% fuel saving
- Types: fire-tube (≤25 t/h), water-tube (>25 t/h), once-through, electric

## Common issues in Thai factories
- **Steam trap failure** — typical 15–30% of traps fail open in old plants → direct steam loss
- **Insulation gaps** — hot bare pipe + valve = up to 1 kW loss per m²
- **Condensate not returning** — running blowdown without recovering hot water
- **Excess air too high** — burner not tuned, easy 3–8% efficiency lift
- **Boiler running at 30% load** — radiation loss eats efficiency

## Retrofit options + ROI
- Steam trap survey + replace: payback 6–12 เดือน
- Insulation upgrade: 1–2 ปี
- Burner tuning + O₂ trim: 6–18 เดือน
- Economizer (preheat feedwater from stack): 1–3 ปี
- Condensate recovery system: 1–2 ปี
- Blowdown heat recovery: 1–2 ปี

## Standards
- **TIS 866** — boiler efficiency (Thailand)
- **ASME I** — Power boilers (pressure vessel)
- **ASME IV** — Heating boilers (low pressure)
- **กฎกระทรวงแรงงาน** — boiler operator certification (กรอ.)
